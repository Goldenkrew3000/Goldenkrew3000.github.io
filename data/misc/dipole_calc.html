Dipole Length Calculator - Malexty.com

This calculator is used for calculating the length of a dipole antenna to tune into a certain frequency for amateur radio. This specific calculator is a javascript based one for online use. All you have to do is type in the desired frequency in MHz into the box, and click Calculate. The calculator will then calculate the length of each dipole, and the whole antenna length in feet and metres.
